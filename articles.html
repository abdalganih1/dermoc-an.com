Frequently Asked Questions

How often can I use the peeling treatment?

We recommend waiting 2-4 weeks between sessions, depending on your skin's sensitivity and response to treatment.

Is the treatment suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our Gentle Protocol is specifically designed for sensitive skin types. Start with the lowest intensity and gradually increase.

How long until I see results?

Many clients see immediate improvement in skin texture. For deeper concerns like pigmentation, 3-6 sessions are typically needed.

Can I use the products at home?

Dermocean products are formulated for professional use in licensed beauty centers. For home care, we recommend our Sunscreen and Calming Mask.
