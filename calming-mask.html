How to Use

1

Cleanse

After peeling treatment, gently remove all residue with warm water.

2

Apply

Apply a generous layer of Calming Mask evenly across the treated area.

3

Wait

Leave the mask on for 15-20 minutes to allow deep absorption.

4

Remove

Gently remove with a damp cloth or rinse with cool water. Follow with SPF.
