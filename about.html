Our Story

Dermocean was born from a vision to revolutionize professional skincare by harnessing the untapped potential of marine biology. Our founders, a team of dermatologists and marine biologists, discovered that specific species of algae contain powerful bioactive compounds that can transform skin at the cellular level.

Today, we are proud to serve hundreds of beauty centers and dermatology clinics across the region, providing them with tools that deliver visible, lasting results for their clients.
