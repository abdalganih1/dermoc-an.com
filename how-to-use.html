Step-by-Step Guide

1

Cleanse the Skin

Thoroughly cleanse the treatment area using a gentle, pH-balanced cleanser. Remove all traces of makeup, oil, and impurities.

Pro Tip: Use lukewarm water to open pores before treatment.
2

Prepare the Mixture

Mix 2g of Algae Peeling Powder with the Support Solution until you achieve a smooth, even paste consistency.

Pro Tip: Use a ceramic or glass bowl - avoid metal containers.
3

Apply Evenly

Using clean fingers or a brush, apply the mixture evenly across the face, avoiding the eye and lip areas.

Pro Tip: Work in sections - forehead, cheeks, nose, chin.
4

Massage

Massage gently in circular motions for the duration specified by your chosen protocol (4-8 minutes).

Pro Tip: Increase pressure gradually on resistant areas like T-zone.
5

Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse the treatment area with lukewarm water until all product residue is removed.

Pro Tip: Pat dry gently - do not rub.
6

Apply Calming Mask

Apply a generous layer of Calming Mask and leave for 15-20 minutes to soothe and hydrate the skin.

Pro Tip: Cover with a damp towel to enhance absorption.
7

Sun Protection

Finish with SPF 50+ sunscreen. This is MANDATORY for the next 7 days post-treatment.

Warning: Skipping sun protection can cause hyperpigmentation!

Precautions

Avoid Sun Exposure

Stay out of direct sunlight for 7 days after treatment.

No Active Acids

Avoid retinol, AHA/BHA for 5 days before and after.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and moisturize regularly.

Treatment Interval

Wait 2-4 weeks between sessions based on skin response.
